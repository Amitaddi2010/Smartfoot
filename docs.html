ü¶∂ Detectable Conditions

1. Normal Foot

Healthy foot structure with proper arch formation and no visible deformities.

2. Flatfoot (Pes Planus)

Condition where the arch of the foot collapses, causing the entire sole to come into contact with the ground.

3. Foot Ulcer

Open wounds or sores on the foot, commonly associated with diabetes or poor circulation.

4. Hallux Valgus (Bunion)

Deformity where the big toe deviates toward the other toes, creating a bony prominence.

üìñ How to Use

Step 1: Upload Image
